PIAIC

PRESEDENTIAL INITIATIVE FOR ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE AND COMPUTING

Presendial Initiative for Artifical Intelligence and Computing (PIAIC) is a privately funded not-for-profit educational initiative started by Presedient Of Pakistan,Dr.Arif Alvi, Kazi Rahat Ali,and Zia Khan to help promote education, research and busniess oppertunities in emerging tech fields like

  • Cloud Native Computing
  • Artifical Intelligence
  • BlockChain
  • Internt Of Things

Cloud Native Computing:

The Cloud Native Computing program prepares the students to program in open source technologies like,Linux,Docker,Kubernetes,Javascript,Node.js,React and React Native while introducing the students to development of microservices and software development techniques on cloud platform like AWS.

Artifical Intelligence

The artifical Intelligence program of PIAIC takes the student through the foundation of the AI technology, via teaching programming in an open source language like python,Numpy,Pandas,Tenserflow 2.0 and Flask, and teaching deploying practical AI solutions and use cases,and ending with real-life examples of modern day AI in practice.

BlockChain:

The Main Emphasis of BlockChain program is to understand the blockchain like Ethereum and Hyperledger(Fabric) and learn to program in langugae such as solidity,javascript on framework like Node.js.The Program also introduces them to development of Distributed Apps (DApps),frameworks like Web3 and Truffle and an in-depth understanding of blockchain-as-a-servicess(Baas) and how to develop and offer an initial Coin Offering (ICO) of a crypto-token.

Internet Of Things

The Internet of things program introduces the student to open source development technolgies like Rust,Embeded Rust,WebAssembly,Hyper,Diesel,Google Assistant and Kubernetes while making them efficient in developing Micro Services Of IOT Edge, IOT Cloud, and voice computing for device control
